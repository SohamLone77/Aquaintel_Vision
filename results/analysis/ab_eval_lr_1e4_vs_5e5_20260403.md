# A/B Evaluation: learning rate 1e-4 vs 5e-5 (50 epochs)

## Setup

- Objective: compare two learning rates with all other settings fixed.
- Loss config: `combined` with `ssim_weight=0.5`.
- Control run: `unet_20260403_0811` with `learning_rate=1e-4`.
- Treatment run: `unet_20260403_1019` with `learning_rate=5e-5`.

## Metrics summary

### Control (`unet_20260403_0811`, 50 epochs)

- Best epoch: `49`
- Best val_loss: `0.066380`
- Best val_mae: `0.072987`
- Final val_loss: `0.070340`

### Treatment (`unet_20260403_1019`, 50 epochs)

- Best epoch: `50`
- Best val_loss: `0.073043`
- Best val_mae: `0.078793`
- Final val_loss: `0.073043`

## Deltas (treatment minus control)

- Best val_loss: `+0.006663` (worse)
- Best val_mae: `+0.005807` (worse)
- Final val_loss: `+0.002703` (worse)
- Final val_mae: `+0.000939` (worse)

## Decision

- Keep `learning_rate=1e-4` as default.
- The `5e-5` run converged more slowly and did not match control within 50 epochs.
